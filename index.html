Introduction

The .SeeMore domain is designed to make every page of your website more compelling with an engaging call to action as your top level domain name.

Blockchain Based

Your domain will exist on the Handshake blockchain, a decentralized system with none of the restrictions imposed by the traditional ICANN domain system.

Claim Your Brand

Get first mover advantage on the blockchain web by claiming your dream domain on .SeeMore and simply redirecting it to your existing ICANN website.

For Engagement

Calls to action are a marketing staple for user engagement. Give every page the benefit of a call to action for categorical information when you choose a .SeeMore domain name.

Use Cases for .SeeMore

Here are some suggested use cases to help you get the most out of your .SeeMore domain name.

Classified Ads

carsforsale.seemore, apartmentsforrent.seemore, homesforsale.seemore

In any category based website, visitors are more likely to arrive through category pages than the home page, .SeeMore enhances every category page on your classified ads website with a compelling call to action to see more. Example: realestate.seemore/2-bedroom-apartments

Directories

doctorsinlocation.seemore, dentistsinlocation.seemore

Directories are some of the most visited sites on the web. People like lists of services. Building a directory with a .SeeMore domain lets people see at a glance that your directory website contains the list of service providers they're looking for.

Jobs Boards

itjobs.seemore/php-developer, recruiter.seemore/it-jobs

People search for jobs using industry and job titles, giving these categories value online. The call to action in the .SeeMore domain panders to prospects browsing by the job title and industry categories of jobs board websites.

Catalogues

artsupplies.seemore, stationary.seemore, auctions.seemore, fashioncatalogue.seemore,

No different to classified ads or directories, .SeeMore domain names make every catalogue section of your website equally important to visitors that are more likely to arrive through your category pages than the home page.

Important Notes

Two important points to note when using handshake domains.

Don't Collect Sensitive Information

SSL is currently not supported by handshake so be mindful of what data you collect via the site if any. Directing users to 3rd party systems to make contact or to process data is recommended. Examples: Click to email, Discord, Telegram, Send a Facebook Message.

Email Is Available Through Hmail

Visit Hmail.app to setup private email on any handshake domain. The current cost is 2.5 hns per month for private email using any handshake domain name you own. Examples john@stationary.shopnow, mary@flowers.shopnow
